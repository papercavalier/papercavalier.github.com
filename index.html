Paper Cavalier[1]

[1]: A hybrid company founded in a decrepit warehouse and based in London and New York.

We own Near Fine, a book-dealing algorithm that trades internationally. The application has so far weathered the subprime crisis and the death of print, doubling revenue every year. Deleuze would have wryly called it a book machine.

Currently, we are busy turning the algorithm inside-out to build a product search site that we ourselves wouldn't mind using.

We <3 printed stuff.
